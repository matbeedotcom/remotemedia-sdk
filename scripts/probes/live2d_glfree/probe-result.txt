VERDICT
-------
live2d-py 0.6.1.1 does NOT expose post-deformer drawable mesh data.
`Update()`/`UpdatePhysics()` compute the deformed mesh internally,
but it is consumed only by `Draw()` (which requires an active GL
context). No Python-level accessor exists for vertex_positions,
vertex_count, indices, UVs, opacity, render_order, or flags.

=> Spec §5 'split Rust+Python backend' is NOT viable on this wrapper.
=> Promote spec §11(1) 'live2d-render-native-wgpu' to first-shipped,
   OR fork live2d-py and add bindings for csmGetDrawable* APIs.
